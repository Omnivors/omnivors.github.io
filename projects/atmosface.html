ATMOSFACE

CONCEPT

The work takes its visual cues from "the face of Mars", hills on Mars that looked like a human face when observed under a particular light. The psychological phenomenon for which the human being tends to perceive a visual or acoustic pattern as something familiar is called paraidolia. The sound design plays with this phenomenon through vocal synthesis. The voices and sounds leave free interpretation to the spectator, who is submerged in an acoustic scenario that recalls the discovery of space.At the same time, ‘Atmosface’ is a mirror to look at and interact with; it reminds us that we are looking for answers and meanings about us elsewhere.

TECHNOLOGIES

Atmosface is an interactive sound installation based on capacitive sensing for pseudo-random sound synthesis over a layered head sculpture. Atmosface was selected for the exihition Making Art 4.0 and will be exhibited in Madrid (ES) from Sep 28th to Oct 7th, 2018.

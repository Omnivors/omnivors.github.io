ATMOSFACE

The challenge in ánemos installation was to create an acoustic environment to blend and cross-reference different artistic disciplines. Photography, poetry and music. We did that with a quadraphonic system that allowed us to link through space the audio cues with photography. ánemos is part of the photographic project by Vita Nolè. and was presented at The Others Fair in Turin (IT), in October 2015.

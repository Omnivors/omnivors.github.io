MUSIC HALL

The space around you is here transformed into a music instrument, and as you pass through the gallery space your movement will produce notes from the Chinese pentatonic scale. The music of your body is played out from the copper cone which floats upon your head. MuscHall was concieved by the artist and poet Alex Cecchetti and exposed as part of At The Gates of the Music Palace exibhition in Spike Island Gallery, Bristol (UK) from May 5 to July 8 2018, and in VOID Gallery, Derry (Northern Ireland) from Aug 4 to Sept 22 2018. We took care of the interaction design process and the whole technical project and realization.

ABOUT

We're a four-headed collective based in Turin (Italy) and Delft (Netherlands). The aim of the project is to design and develop interactive installations, sculptures, videos, musical instruments and any sort of artistic work that relies on electronics and digital technologies.

We believe in sharing knowledge and expertise, DIY practices and open source. Furthermore, the interest in cultural and artistic projects goes hand in hand with a passion for technical development and scientific research.

WHAT WE DO

Our common background in computer technology, electronics, music, art and design is the driving force that gave birth to Omnivors! We deal with several fields. We eat everything!

interaction design // digital media // sound design // 3D modeling and printing // generative video // web audio // VFX // VR and 360video
